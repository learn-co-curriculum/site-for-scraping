Introduction to Data Science

Introduction to Data Science gives students better tools to make data even more meaningful.

This part time course provides students with the skills to extract meaning from datasets too large for Excel. Over the course of two weekends, students will learn to gain customer insights by analyzing a public dataset with hundreds of thousands of records using the pandas library and Python.

All Data Science students should feel comfortable using Excel for basic statistics. This includes concepts such as mean, mode, median, outliers and normal distributions. For students that are not familiar with Python, there will be pre-work to bring them up to the level of understanding necessary for the course. If you have any questions, please contact us at info@flatironschool.com.
