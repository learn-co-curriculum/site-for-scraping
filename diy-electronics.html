DIY Electronics - Build Your Own Sound Generating Circuit

DIY Electronics - Build A Sound Generating Circuit

Build a sound-generating circuit from scratch! This beginner-friendly workshop will have students learning the fundamentals of electricty, reading circuit diagrams and soldering. At the end of class, you will have your own Atari Punk Console that you you built from scratch. You can use it to to impress family & friends, to prank co-workers, or even to add a new instrument to your avant garde electronic band. Cost includes all materials for the class. Just come ready to learn!




Curriculum

Learn the hardware skills necessary to build a functional sound circuit.

Electricity

Learn the basics of electricity and how to calculate: current, voltage, resistance, series vs parallel, and Ohms law.

Circuit Diagrams

Learn how to read an write electronics diagrams that engineers use to plan and design real circuits.

Soldering

Learn how to solder components onto a PCB (printed circuit board).
