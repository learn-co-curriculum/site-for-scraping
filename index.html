Student Projects

Heat Seek NYC

Heat Seek NYC helps New Yorkers validate legal claims against landlords who won't keep the heat on. These temperature sensors are now in real homes, thanks to a fully-backed Kickstarter, an NYC Big Apps win, and a shoutout out from Mayor de Blasio.

by William Jeffries, Tristan Siegel, and Daniel Kronovet

Kickammender

Kickammender is a recommendation engine for Kickstarter. It gets to know its users with an algorithm. Then it recommends projects for them to back based on their interests.

by Michael Polycarpou and Joe O'Conor

Canyon of Heroes

Built in partnership with the Downtown Alliance, this iOS app celebrates Lower Manhattan's historic ticker-tape parades. Now available in the App Store.

by Ray Milian, Peter Prosol, and Viktor Falkner
